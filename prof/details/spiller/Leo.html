Leo

Traits to Focus On

  • Individuality
  • Being ready to take the "center stage"
  • Following heart's desires
  • Strengthening will
  • Self confidence
  • Being happy
  • Taking risks
  • Connecting with the child in other people
  • Enjoying life, having fun
  • Seeing life as a game
  • Developing a sense of "this depends on me" attitude

Traits to Leave Behind

  • Obeying others in order to fit in
  • Detaching self from emotional situations
  • Being cold, emotionally unavailable
  • Requiring a push from others in order to start acting
  • Losing sight of what is really going on
  • Always waiting for "more information" before acting
  • Being stuck in dreamland
  • Avoiding confrontations

The Achilles Heel of such people is their overdeveloped need to fit in and be accepted by their peers. This person must be their own best friend and push themselves to seek things that will bring them happiness. They also need to stay away from the trap of constantly looking for more knowledge. They will never find enough of it to the degree that will force them into action anyway; so instead they need to take charge and move forward in a direction that will bring them their own personal happiness.
