Virgo

Traits to Focus On

  • Participation
  • Bringing order to chaos
  • Creating and following routines
  • Focusing on here and now
  • Being compassionate
  • Servicing others
  • Analyzing and categorizing
  • Gaining a sense of self worth by gaining experience
  • Being mild, genial
  • Taking risks in spite of fear
  • Noticing details and giving importance to them

Traits to Leave Behind

  • Seeing self as the "wronged person", always being in this frame of mind
  • Being confused and losing direction
  • Avoding making plans
  • A tendency of escaping from reality, possibility of substance abuse (alcohol, drugs)
  • Being exorbiant, excessive, immoderate
  • Not trusting oneself and one's skills
  • A feeling of inadequency, insufficiency
  • Being over-sensitive
  • Retreating
  • Being non-commital, not wanting to take part, being immobile
  • Giving up

The Achilles Heel of these people is their sense of victimization, of being wronged. They need to give up searching for a guru, a coach that they want to surrender to completely, because this search is a bottomless hole. Life isn't organized in a way to let them do this. What they need to understand is organizing their life in a way so that the outcomes they want will occur naturally. For this, they must actively participate in life.
