Poached Mackarel with Leeks in Cream Sauce

Ingredients (for 6)

  • 4 mackerel (300 g each)
  • 1/2 litre white wine
  • 4 leeks (white parts only)
  • 100 g low-fat creme fraiche
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper

    • Description

    Clean the mackerel. Heat the white wine and poach the mackerel in it for 5 minutes.
