Homemade Natural Deodorant (Do Not Eat)

Ingredients

  • ½ cup baking soda (¼ cup for sensitive skin)
  • ½ cup arrowroot powder (or cornstarch)
  • 5 tablespoons unrefined coconut oil, melted or softened
  • 20 drops essential oil (grapefruit, tea tree, etc.)

    • Description

  • Mix the baking soda and arrowroot powder together in a bowl until combined.
  • Add the coconut oil and essential oils to the mixture. Mix well until a uniform paste forms.
  • Pour into a clean, airtight glass jar and store at room temperature (or in a cool cupboard in warm climates).
  • Use a popsicle stick or finger to scoop out a pea-sized amount and apply under each arm.
