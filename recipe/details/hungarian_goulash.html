Hungarian Goulash

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons oil (divided)
  • Onions
  • Caraway seeds
  • Spicy paprika
  • Sweet paprika
  • Garlic
  • Beef
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Bay leaf
  • Tomato paste
  • Beef broth

    • Description

  • Warm 1 tablespoon of the oil in a large heavy soup pot over medium heat. Add the onions, caraway seeds, spicy and sweet paprika and cook, stirring occasionally, until soft, about 7 minutes.
  • Add the garlic and continue to cook for 1 minute. Remove the mixture from the pan, place in a bowl, and set aside.
  • In a bowl, dust the beef with flour and a large pinch of salt and pepper.
  • Add the remaining 2 tablespoons of oil to the pan. Increase heat to medium and cook the beef in batches until golden on all sides, about 8 to 10 minutes per batch.
  • Add the onion mixture, bay leaf, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, tomato paste, and beef broth to the pan.
  • Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat.
  • Reduce the heat to low and simmer covered, stirring occasionally, until the beef is tender and can be easily skewered with a fork, about 2 to 2 1/2 hours.
