Turkey Stew

Ingredients

  • 150 g turkey meat
  • 250 g peas (about 10 tablespoons)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt
  • Cumin

    • Description

  • First, heat the olive oil slightly and add the turkey meat.
  • After saut√©ing for 10 minutes, add the tomato paste, and the onion and red pepper, which have been diced.
  • Once the onion and red pepper have softened, add the chopped garlic, grated tomatoes, peas, and 1 cup of water.
  • Cover the pot and let the mixture cook.
  • Just before you finish cooking, add salt and about half a teaspoon of cumin, stir, and your meal is ready.
