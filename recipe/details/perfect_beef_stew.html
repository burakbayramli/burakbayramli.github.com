Perfect Beew Stew

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Beef chuck
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Red Wine
  • Cornstarch
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Pepper

    • Description

  • Mix the beef chuck with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, let it marinade for an hour.
  • Sear the beef on both sides in pot in olive oil. Remove the beef from the pot.
  • Pour diced onions in the pot (now full of juices left over from searing), add salt, and caramalize the onion. Then add the diced celery, carrots, garlic. Saute all of that until fragrant.
  • Next add tomato paste, and red wine.
  • Bring to simmer, then add the beef back in the pot. Add herbs - rosemarry, thyme, bay leaf. Add water, season with more salt and pepper. Cover and let it cook for 1.5 hours.
  • Add more carrots and potatoes, let it simmer for another 1.5 hours. Once the beef is tender and the vegetables are soft, add cornstarch to thicken the stew.
